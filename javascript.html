Ariya the Persistant Butterfly

This game was made from the W3 Schools JavaScript tutorial. I enhanced the game by making it larger, adding CSS, and a background image.

As a test of strength, Ariya must journey into the treacherous magical forest and overcome any obstacles that stand in her path. Can you help Ariya to spread her wings?
Use the FLY button to stay in the air.
